Notion Job Saver

Save LinkedIn jobs to your Notion database in one click.

Create one at notion.so/my-integrations, then share your database with it (••• → Connections). Open your DB as a full page and copy the URL — the ID is extracted automatically.
Setup steps
  1. Create an internal integration at notion.so/my-integrations and copy its token.
  2. Open your jobs database → ••• → Connections → add your integration.
  3. Copy the database page URL and paste it above.
  4. Hit Test connection — it confirms access and lists your columns.
